About Untriggered

Untriggered aims to protect internet users from unwelcome content that may keep them from having an enjoyable and safe online experience. Out program allows users to select words that may trigger unwelcome memories or emotions, while still giving the user the ultimate decision.


This extension was made by Shreya Patel and Ivey Dyson - two University of Oklahoma students - for Hack_UTD 2017!


We hope Untriggered makes your internet experience more enjoyable.
